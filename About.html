Who I am?

Asim Jofa burst on to the Fashion scene as a breath of fresh air.
Carrying on the legacy of his forefathers he started with a jewelry
line 12 years ago,establishing the highly exclusive, one of a kind
diamond boutique ‘The Diamond Gallery’.Having been certified in
Design from the prestigious Central Saint Martins, Asim went on
to create ripples in the fashion industry. He has achieved great
acclaim for his various collections displayed on the runways in
the fashion capitals of the world.
